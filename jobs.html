Jobs

Our graduate students are frequently hired for Teaching Assistant (TA) positions in the following departments/faculties:

  • The Department of Statistical Sciences, University of Toronto (St. George)
  • The Department of Mathematical and Computational Sciences, University of Toronto (Mississauga)
  • The Department of Computer and Mathematical Science, University of Toronto (Scarborough)
  • The Dalla Lana School of Public Health, University of Toronto

Deadlines for Fall/Winter TA applications are typically in mid-July, although positions may be posted throughout the year. A full list of CUPE Local 3902 Unit 1 positions and instructions on how to apply is available here.
