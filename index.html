About BUGS

The Biostatistics Union of Graduate Students (BUGS) was founded by biostatistics students for biostatistics students. Our mandate is to represent the interests of the students within our department and the school, as well as to provide a sense of community for our students. Our executive is made up of student volunteers and our monthly meetings are open to all students who wish to help out.
